ABOUT US

Since 1993, our company is specializing in air, sea, truck, and ships spares freight forwarding activities. Our offices are located at Piraeus and since then, our name has been synonymous to reliability, flexibility and effectiveness regarding the goods transportation, enjoying an excellent reputation towards our clients.
Our team is highly trained and experienced, aiming to offer a complete service with the best, most effective solutions with extremely competitive rates.

OUR EXPERIENCE

enables us to cope with any challenge and achieve the best possible results

OUR PARTNERS

consist of most airlines, shipping and land transportation companies, having their own stock of airway bills. A fairly large network of representatives worldwide cooperate with us to facilitate the process of offering the best services to our clients.

OUR CLIENTS

are Large Shipping Companies, very well-known commercial enterprises, Radio & TV Stations, as well as import and export companies trusting us to support them with their freights and customs clearance processes.

AIR/OCEAN/LAND TRANSPORTATION

  • AIR TRANSPORT
    Our company cooperates with all airlines using the appropriate and most reliable flights based on the needs of our customers and the quality of their goods.
